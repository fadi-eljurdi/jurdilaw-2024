Divorce and Inheritance

Navigating the emotional and legal complexities of divorce and inheritance can be particularly challenging for members of the Lebanese diaspora, who often face cross-jurisdictional hurdles. At Jurdi & Co, we offer specialized and empathetic legal support to Lebanese individuals and families residing abroad.


Our expertise lies in resolving cross-border family law matters, encompassing divorce proceedings, child custody issues, property division, succession rights, and meticulous estate management in Lebanon. We are committed to safeguarding our clients' rights and obligations under Lebanese law, while always considering their unique circumstances abroad.


From verifying wills and executing estates to managing disputes among heirs, our team handles sensitive family matters with the utmost discretion, empathy, and legal precision. Our aim is to provide clear legal guidance and practical solutions, empowering Lebanese expatriates to resolve personal issues with confidence and peace of mind, effectively bridging the gap between their legal needs abroad and the Lebanese legal framework.
