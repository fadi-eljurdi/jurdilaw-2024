Gaming & Esports

In navigating the legal complexities of game creation, distribution, and esports, we are here to guide our clients. Our expert legal team specialises in gaming law, offering comprehensive services for both game developers and players. We assist with all aspects of game law, including intellectual property (IP) protection, technology, regulatory and compliance. We also assist clients in relation to digital rights, consumer protection laws, and privacy regulations, including data protection and privacy compliance for online games. In addition, Jurdi & Co provide legal counsel for esports organisations, including tournament organisers, players, and sponsors. Whether our clients are dealing with sponsorship contracts, player agreements, dispute resolution, or regulatory issues relating to esports competitions, our team is equipped to protect our clients interests in this rapidly growing industry. With our team of deep industry knowledge and a passion for gaming, we help our clients navigate the complex legal ecosystem of the gaming world, so clients can focus on creating, developing, and expanding your gaming business or esports career.
