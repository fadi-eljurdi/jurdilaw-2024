Legal Services for Game Developers & Publishers in Lebanon


We assist independent studios, established developers, and publishers with all legal aspects of gaming law, ensuring their products, platforms, and operations are legally secure and compliant. Our services include:


  • Intellectual Property (IP) Protection: Comprehensive strategies for IP protection including copyrights for game content, artwork, music, and narratives; trademarks for game titles and branding; and patents for unique game mechanics or technology.
  • Licensing & Distribution Agreements: Drafting and negotiating agreements for distribution platforms (e.g., app stores, console platforms), in-game assets, third-party content, and middleware.
  • Data Protection & Privacy Compliance: Ensuring full compliance with Lebanese data privacy laws (such as Law No. 81/2018) and international regulations (e.g., GDPR) for user data, online gaming, and mobile applications.
  • Consumer Protection & Digital Marketplaces: Advising on consumer protection laws specific to digital goods and services, age restrictions, in-game purchases, and terms of service for online marketplaces.
  • Technology Transactions: Structuring software development agreements, cloud service contracts, and other essential technology transactions.
  • Pre-Launch Legal Strategy: From initial concept to live operations, we support game creators at every stage of the development and publishing cycle, including localization legal review.
