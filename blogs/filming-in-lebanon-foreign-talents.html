?Chapter One: Foreign Talents - Part 1


Employment of foreign artists is addressed in the Law 58/2008, relating to the organising of artistic professions in Lebanon ("Law"), under which an artist is defined as:  


"A natural person performing in his/her capacity as a creator or performer of a certain role in one of the arts categories listed under the Law.


This includes performing or acting in a film or other audio-visual work. The Law addresses artists (in our case performers), desiring to work in Lebanon in one of the "Artistic Professions" stipulated in the Law in consideration of a remuneration. Foreign artists must, for the purpose of obtaining a work permit, obtain from the Ministry of Culture (MoC) a certificate of reciprocity confirming that lebanese artists are receiving same treatment in the country which the artist is a national of, as well as a confirmation from the MoC presenting that this practice does not damage the interests of Lebanese artists. 


On the other hand, Article Two of the Directive 17561/1964 relating to the organising of foreign labour, presents that every foreigner desiring to enter to Lebanon for the purpose of working or performing a job (paid or not) is required to procure the approval of the Ministry of Labour (MoL), prior to the worker's/employee's arrival to Lebanon, unless he/she is an artist, then such approval shall be procured from the Lebanese Directorate of General Security.


The Directorate of General Security conditions that foreign artists cannot enter to Lebanon for work, except with a prior ?artist? entry visa issued by the Artists Department and based on a request submitted to General Security by the inviting party (entity or person) specifying the date, duration and place of work, in accordance with the established procedures. Any person/entity interested in hiring a foreign artist ("Applicant") can apply for an expedited visa process to the Artists Department with the complete documents and the Artists' Department will conclude the visa process within a period of 48 hours.
