FILMING IN LEBANON - TAXES

Setting the stage for a successful production project in Lebanon requires meticulous planning, especially regarding tax implications. While structuring your project solely to minimize taxes is strongly discouraged, overlooking potential tax liabilities can be equally detrimental.


This article serves as a preliminary checklist for navigating Lebanon's tax landscape, particularly for productions with cross-territorial elements. Remember, consulting with your tax advisor and production lawyers is paramount before making any decisions.


A. Tax Considerations for Filming in Lebanon:


  1. Corporate Income Tax (Risk of Permanent Establishment): Avoid inadvertently establishing a "permanent establishment" through extensive and repetitive engagement in local activities like hiring local crew through employment contracts, having resident representatives and promoting your services and goods in Lebanon while you have no legal presence there. This could subject you to Lebanese corporate income tax, currently set at 17%.
  2. Value Added Tax (VAT): Unlike other jurisdictions, recovering VAT in Lebanon can be a cumbersome process. Factoring potential VAT costs into your budget upfront is crucial. The standard VAT rate in Lebanon is currently 11%.
  3. Withholding Tax: Cross-border payments to vendors or receiving payments as a foreign business can trigger withholding tax obligations. Plan for potential budget overages. Withholding tax rates in Lebanon vary depending on the type of payment, example:


  • Non-Resident Withholding Tax (WHT) on goods: 3.4%
  • Non-Resident Withholding Tax (WHT) on services: 8.5%


Remember: While tax optimization is a legitimate strategy, tax evasion is a serious offense. Ensure your business structure is driven by sound business interests, not solely by minimizing taxes.


Tax Rates (Lebanon) - 2024 


Corporate Income Tax: 17%

Value Added Tax (VAT): 11% (Standard Rate)

Withholding Tax Rates:

Non-Resident WHT (Goods): 3.4%

Non-Resident WHT (Services): 8.5%

Dividend Distribution WHT: 10%

Withholding Tax on Interest Income: 7%
