Filming With Military Equipment & Personnel

A. Filming with Military Equipment in Lebanon: Requirements and Procedures


Lebanon's diverse terrain and historical significance make it an attractive location for filmmakers. If your production requires the use of military equipment for stunts or dramatic effects, there are specific procedures and permissions you must obtain.


B. Application Process


The Lebanese Armed Forces oversee the use of military equipment in film productions. For that purpose they require a detailed application that includes:


  • Beneficiary: clearly indicate the production company, institution, or individual responsible for the filming.
  • Purpose of Filming: describe the nature of the project (movie, documentary, commercial, etc.) and how the military equipment will be used.
  • Filming Details: provide the filming location(s) and the exact dates and times the equipment will be needed.
  • Military Assistance: specify the type of assistance needed
  • Personnel: (soldiers, technical advisors)
  • Equipment: (weapons, vehicles, aircraft)
  • Detailed Schedule: include a breakdown of when and where specific military equipment will be featured.
  • Documents: attach all relevant supporting documents (scripts, production plans, etc.).
  • Contact Information: include your address and phone number.


C. Additional Considerations


  • Approval: applications will be reviewed by the Lebanese Armed Forces. Approval is not guaranteed and may take a significant amount of time to process.
  • Security: the Lebanese Armed Forces will likely place restrictions on filming locations and activities to ensure safety and national security.
  • Costs: you may be responsible for certain fees/expenses incurred in providing military personnel or equipment.


Important Note: Filming regulations are subject to change. Always contact the appropriate Lebanese authorities for the most up-to-date information before commencing your project.
